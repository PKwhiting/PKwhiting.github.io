Site Name

The Weather Genie

Site Domain

theweathergenie.com

Site Purpose

The main purpose of The Weather Genie is to give live and accurate weather information to individuals that live within Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven Idaho. Residents of the covered communities will be able to quickly view and understand the weather in their area. The website will also include articles and forecasts for not only current weather, but the weather for upcoming weeks. Additionally, the website will provide basic coverage of local news and will display upcoming events in the community. The Weather Genie will allow for individuals to connect more deeply with their community through the news and these events. The upcoming events, news, and weather will allow for residents of the covered areas to have a one stop shop to inform themselves about what is going on in their area.
