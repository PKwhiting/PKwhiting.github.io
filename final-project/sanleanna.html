San Leanna

San Leanna is a small town southwest of Austin. Settled in the 1840's this small town is home to a small population of 676. It is surrounded by many other small towns and has a high school, middle school, and elementary school all in close proximity. San Leanna is a small bustling town full of food trucks, barbecue and bars. A beautiful little piece of heaven in the heart of Texas.

Demographics

  • Population: 676
  • Housing: 153 Units
  • 90.62% White
  • Median Age: 44
